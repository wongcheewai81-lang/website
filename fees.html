Costs

Retinal surgery costs in Singapore

The total cost of retinal surgery depends on the complexity of the procedure, the type of anaesthesia, hospital charges, and whether Medisave or insurance is used. Below is a general guide to what is involved in the fee structure ‚Äî please contact the clinic for a personalised estimate.

What's included in the fee estimate

  • Surgeon's fee ‚Äî covers the procedure, pre-operative assessment, and post-operative follow-up visits
  • Anaesthesia ‚Äî most vitreoretinal surgeries are performed under local anaesthesia; general anaesthesia incurs additional cost
  • Hospital facility fee ‚Äî day surgery centre charges at Gleneagles Hospital, including the operating theatre and recovery room
  • Consumables ‚Äî surgical instruments, intraocular gas or silicone oil tamponade, laser probes, and other disposables
  • Medications ‚Äî post-operative eye drops and any intraoperative drugs

Factors that affect cost

  • Procedure complexity ‚Äî a straightforward vitrectomy for epiretinal membrane costs less than a complex retinal detachment repair with silicone oil
  • Combined procedures ‚Äî some cases require cataract surgery at the same sitting, adding to the total
  • Tamponade type ‚Äî gas tamponade is included in most cases; silicone oil adds consumable cost and requires a second procedure for removal
  • Length of stay ‚Äî most retinal surgeries are day surgery, but complex cases may require overnight observation

MOH publishes fee benchmarks for common surgical procedures at private hospitals, including vitreoretinal surgeries. These benchmarks show the typical range of total bills (surgeon fee + hospital charges) and can help you set expectations before your consultation.

Billing

What to expect

Consultation fees

First consultation and follow-up fees are provided at the time of booking. A fee estimate will be shared before any procedure. Contact the clinic for current consultation rates.

Surgical estimates

Before any surgery, you will receive a written fee estimate covering surgeon fees, anaesthesia, hospital charges, and consumables. You will have time to review and clarify any items.

Medisave & insurance claims

The clinic team will assist with Medisave withdrawal forms and liaise with your insurer for pre-authorisation letters and direct billing where applicable. Please bring your insurance documents to your appointment.
