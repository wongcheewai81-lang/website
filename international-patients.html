01

Telemedicine

Virtual consultation available

Consult Dr. Wong before you travel

Dr. Wong offers telemedicine consultations for overseas patients who want a professional assessment before committing to travel. During the virtual session, Dr. Wong will review your scans and medical history, explain your condition and treatment options, and advise whether surgery in Singapore is recommended.

This helps you make an informed decision and plan your trip with confidence. To arrange a virtual consultation, contact the clinic via WhatsApp or email with your medical records.

Practical Information

Planning your trip to Singapore

Everything you need to know before travelling for eye treatment.

Visa information

Citizens of most ASEAN countries — including Indonesia, Vietnam, and Cambodia — do not require a visa for short stays in Singapore (up to 30 days). For other nationalities, check with the Immigration & Checkpoints Authority of Singapore (ICA) before booking.

Accommodation near Gleneagles

  • Shangri-La Hotel — 5 min walk
  • The St. Regis Singapore — 5 min walk
  • Hotel Jen Tanglin — 3 min walk
  • Many serviced apartments also available nearby

Length of stay

  • Intravitreal injections: 1–2 days
  • Cataract surgery: 2–3 days
  • Vitrectomy / retinal detachment surgery: 5–7 days
  • Complex or combined procedures: 7–10 days

Payment & insurance

Medisave is not applicable for international patients. However, many international insurance plans are accepted at Gleneagles Hospital. Self-pay patients are welcome — transparent, all-inclusive pricing is discussed before treatment so there are no surprises.
